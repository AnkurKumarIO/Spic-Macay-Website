Featured Guru

Hindustani Instrumental

Pt. Hariprasad Chaurasia

Bansuri Maestro · Padma Vibhushan

One of India's most celebrated flutists, Pandit Hariprasad Chaurasia has elevated the bansuri to global prominence. His music is a seamless weave of deep classical tradition and lyrical beauty, carrying the essence of ancient India into every performance.

He will be conducting an intimate Baithak session as well as an evening concert during the convention, offering participants a rare opportunity to witness a living legend in an immersive, ashram-like setting.
