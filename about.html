About SPIC MACAY

Society for the Promotion of Indian Classical Music And Culture Amongst Youth — a voluntary youth movement since 1977.

About the Convention

The SPIC MACAY International Convention is a flagship gathering that brings together renowned maestros, young learners, and cultural enthusiasts for a transformative experience. The convention features dawn-to-dusk activities — from Naad Yoga and Hatha Yoga in the morning to classical music and dance performances in the evening.

Workshops across music, dance, craft, and cinema classics run throughout the day. Heritage walks and baithaks offer intimate interactions with artists, while seva and volunteering foster community spirit. The ashram-like environment encourages participants to disconnect from the noise of daily life and reconnect with the timeless wisdom embedded in India's classical traditions.

The Visionary

Dr. Kiran Seth

Dr. Kiran Seth founded SPIC MACAY in 1977 as a student of IIT Delhi, driven by a profound concern that India's youth were drifting away from their extraordinary cultural heritage. What began as a small series of classical music programmes at IIT Delhi has grown into a global movement.

A Professor Emeritus of Mechanical Engineering, Dr. Seth has dedicated his life — beyond his academic responsibilities — to ensuring that India's intangible cultural heritage reaches every corner of the country. His philosophy is simple: expose young people to the depth and beauty of classical arts, and they will be transformed.

Dr. Seth was awarded the prestigious Padma Shri in 2009 in recognition of his extraordinary contribution to Indian classical arts. He continues to serve as the guiding spirit and tireless driving force behind SPIC MACAY's mission.

HOST INSTITUTION

Indian Institute of Technology
Kharagpur

VNIT Nagpur (Visvesvaraya National Institute of Technology), established in 1960, is one of India's premier NITs. Spread across a lush 225-acre campus on South Ambazari Road, Nagpur, it is home to a vibrant student community with a rich tradition in arts, culture, and innovation.

The campus provides the ideal setting for SPIC MACAY's immersive convention — with spacious lawns for dawn yoga, large auditoria for concerts, heritage buildings for walks, and hostels for accommodation. IIT-KGP's student community is one of the most active SPIC MACAY chapters in the country.
