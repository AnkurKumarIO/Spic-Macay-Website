The Flagship Festival

Embrace Your Virasat

Virasat is not just a festival; it is a profound journey into the heart of India’s classical heritage. Held annually at the VNIT Nagpur campus, this 3-day immersion brings legendary maestros, intricate art forms, and ancient wisdom directly to the youth.

Dawn to Dusk Immersions: Experience everything from Naad Yoga at sunrise to spellbinding all-night classical concerts.
Guru-Shishya Parampara: Intimate baithaks and interactive workshops bridging the gap between artist and student.
Holistic Arts: Classical music, dance forms, heritage walks, folk arts, and craft workshops.

LEARNING & EXPERIENCES

Upcoming Events & Workshops

Dive hands-on into India's profound artistic lineages with interactive daytime educational modules led by renowned experts.

Classical Instrument Workshops

Understand the intricate structures of Hindustani and Carnatic string and percussion systems through guided, practical modules in Sitar, Tabla, Flute, and Violin.

Traditional Art Masterclasses

Connect with India's rich visual heritage. Master ancient techniques of Madhubani painting and Warli tribal art under national award-winning master craftsmen.

Guru-Shishya Lecture-Demos

Immerse yourself in interactive lecture-demonstrations that break down complex music theories, rhythmic patterns, and historical backgrounds behind iconic art forms.
