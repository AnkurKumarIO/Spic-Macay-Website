ACM Student Chapter
VNIT Nagpur

The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) Student Chapter at VNIT Nagpur is the campus’s premier computing and technology hub. Dedicated to instilling a robust research mindset and fostering a progressive software engineering culture, the chapter empowers developers, hosts state-level hackathons, and designs full-stack digital portals to solve real-world community challenges.
