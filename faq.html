Everything you need to know about attending the Spic Macay VNIT Nagpur.

🔍
📝 Registration
Visit the Register page and complete the three-step form. You'll receive a confirmation email with your unique Registration ID within 24 hours of submission.
Yes! SPIC MACAY warmly welcomes participants from across the globe. International delegates should apply early and may need to arrange Indian visas independently. Contact registrations@spicmacay.com for international delegate support.
Yes. Choose the "Institution" option during registration. A single faculty coordinator can manage multiple student delegates under one account. Each delegate will receive their individual ID upon approval.
The convention is organised on a nominal cost basis to cover accommodation, meals, and logistics. Scholarship provisions are available for deserving students. Please contact the team for fee details and scholarship applications.
Registration closes once capacity is reached or on 15th May 2026, whichever is earlier. Apply early — past conventions have filled up weeks in advance. Volunteer applications remain open until 20th May 2026.
🏠 Accommodation & Meals
Participants will be housed in the hostels of VNIT Nagpur. Accommodation is arranged in shared dormitory-style rooms, fostering a community living experience consistent with the ashram ethos of the convention.
All meals are vegetarian and holistically curated — nourishing, simple, and sattvic in nature. Breakfast, lunch, and dinner are provided. Jain and vegan options can be requested during registration. Outside food is discouraged during the convention.
Arrival: Strictly between 4:00 PM on 24th May and 7:00 AM on 25th May 2026.
Departure: Strictly between 2:00 PM and 11:00 PM on 31st May 2026.
Please do not arrive or depart outside these windows as campus access is restricted.
📅 Schedule & Activities
Yes, Hatha Yoga / Naad Yoga at 4:00 AM is a core part of the convention experience and is expected of all participants (except for medical reasons). It is one of the most transformative aspects of the week — past participants consistently describe it as life-changing.
Participants are assigned to learning streams based on their preferences submitted during registration. You may indicate your preferred streams (music, dance, craft, film) and we do our best to accommodate your choices. Flexibility within the programme is allowed during afternoon open sessions.
The Overnight Concert on the night of 30th May (Day 6) runs from 8:45 PM to 6:00 AM — an unbroken, spiritually charged cycle of classical music culminating in the sacred dawn ragas. It is the highlight of every convention. Sleeping is respectfully discouraged during this time!
Shramdaan (literal: donation of labour) is a 15-minute collective cleaning of the campus every morning. It reflects the Gandhian values of self-reliance, humility, and community spirit that are central to SPIC MACAY's philosophy.
🚂 Travel & Getting There
By Train: Nagpur Junction Railway Station is well-connected to major cities including Mumbai, Delhi, Kolkata, Chennai, Pune, and Bangalore. The VNIT campus is approximately 6 km from the station — Nagpur Metro, auto-rickshaws, and cabs are readily available.

By Air: Fly directly into Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar International Airport, Nagpur (NAG), which is located just 7 km from the VNIT campus. Direct prepaid cabs, autos, and metro links connect the airport to the campus.

Detailed travel guidelines will be shared after registration is confirmed.
Yes. Pick-up assistance will be available at Nagpur Junction Railway Station during the designated arrival window (4 PM–7 AM). Participants traveling in groups are encouraged to coordinate arrival times and share transport. Details will be communicated after registration.
🤝 Volunteering
Volunteers are the backbone of the convention. Roles include logistics coordination, hospitality, artist liaison, media documentation, technical support, and seva (community service). Volunteering is a deeply fulfilling experience and volunteers get full access to all events.
Yes. Volunteers receive campus accommodation, all meals, and full access to all sessions and concerts — complimentary. Register as a Volunteer on the registration page.
