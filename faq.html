Yes, Hatha Yoga / Naad Yoga at 4:00 AM is a core part of the convention experience and is expected of all participants (except for medical reasons). It is one of the most transformative aspects of the week — past participants consistently describe it as life-changing.
Shramdaan (literal: donation of labour) is a 15-minute collective cleaning of the campus every morning. It reflects the Gandhian values of self-reliance, humility, and community spirit that are central to SPIC MACAY's philosophy.
🤝 Volunteering
Volunteers are the backbone of the convention. Roles include logistics coordination, hospitality, artist liaison, media documentation, technical support, and seva (community service). Volunteering is a deeply fulfilling experience and volunteers get full access to all events.
