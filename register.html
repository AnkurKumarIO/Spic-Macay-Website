Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

When you register for an event, we collect personal information necessary to process your registration and ensure a smooth experience. This includes:

Identity & Contact Data: Full Name, VNIT Roll Number/ID, Email Address, and Mobile Number.

⚠️ We do not collect registration fees — so never share your bank account details, UPI PINs, or credit/debit card numbers to anyone if asked, regarding our events.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data strictly for the following purpose:

  • To confirm your registration and issue event passes/receipts.

3. Data Security and Sharing

Your privacy is paramount. In alignment with VNIT Nagpur's data policies:

  • We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for commercial or marketing purposes.
  • Data is only accessible to authorized SPIC MACAY VNIT organizing committee members.
